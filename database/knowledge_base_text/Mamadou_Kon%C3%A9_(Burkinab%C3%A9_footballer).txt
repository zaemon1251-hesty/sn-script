Mamadou Koné (born 6 March 1974) is a Burkinabé former footballer who played as a defender for US des Forces Armées and EF Ouagadougou. He made 30 appearances for the Burkina Faso national team from 1995 to 2000.[2] He was also named in Burkina Faso's squad for the 1996 African Cup of Nations tournament.[3]
