Romain Brégerie (born 9 August 1986) is a French footballer who plays as a centre-back.[1]
Career
[edit]After moving from FC Girondins de Bordeaux in summer 2008 to FC Metz, the defender was loaned to LB Châteauroux in January 2010. In June 2011 he signed a two-year contract with Dynamo Dresden.[2] On 4 July 2014, he signed a one-year contract with SV Darmstadt 98.[3] In January 2018, he returned to Darmstadt, joining on loan from Ingolstadt 04 for the second half of the season.[4]
