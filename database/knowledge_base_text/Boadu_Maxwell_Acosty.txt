Boadu Maxwell Acosty (born 10 September 1991) is a Ghanaian professional footballer who plays as a winger.[2]
Career
[edit]Reggiana
[edit]Acosty started his youth and senior career with Italian club Reggiana in the 2008–09 Lega Pro Prima Divisione season before going on to sign for Fiorentina in July 2009.[citation needed]
Fiorentina
[edit]Acosty made his Serie A debut for Fiorentina on 22 January 2012, in a match against Cagliari when he came on as a substitute in the 64th minute for teammate Adem Ljajić.[3]
FC Anyang
[edit]After having played for HNK Rijeka in the Croatian First Football League between 2017 and 2020, Acosty joined Korean second division side FC Anyang in February 2020 as free transfer.[4]
