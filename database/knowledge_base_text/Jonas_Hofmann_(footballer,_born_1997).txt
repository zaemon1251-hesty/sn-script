Jonas Hofmann (born 7 February 1997) is a German footballer who plays as a midfielder for Energie Cottbus.[2] He made his debut for the Schalke 04 first-team in 2020, coming off the bench against Bayer Leverkusen in a 1-1 draw.[3]
