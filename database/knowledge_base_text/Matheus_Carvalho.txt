Matheus Thiago de Carvalho (born March 11, 1992), known as Matheus Carvalho, is a Brazilian footballer who plays for Barra FC.[1][2]
Career
[edit]Fluminense
[edit]Matheus made his league debut for Fluminense against Atlético Goianiense on 29 May 2011.[3] He scored his first goal for the club against Coritiba on 16 July 2011, scoring in the 78th minute.[4]
Joinville
[edit]Matheus made his league debut for Joinville against Criciúma on 17 February 2013.[5] He scored his first goal for Joinville against Juventus SC on 23 February 2013, scoring in the 60th minute.[6]
AS Monaco
[edit]On 31 January 2015, Carvalho moved on loan to AS Monaco FC till the end of the 2014–15 season, with an option for AS Monaco to make the deal permanent.[7] He made his debut for AS Monaco against Nice on 20 February 2015.[8]
On 13 March 2015, Carvalho scored his first goal for AS Monaco FC vs Bastia.[9]
Fort Lauderdale Strikers
[edit]Carvalho signed with North American Soccer League club Fort Lauderdale Strikers on 8 February 2016.[10] He made his debut for Fort Lauderdale Strikers against Minnesota United on 17 April 2016.[11]
Atlético Goianiense
[edit]Matheus made his league debut for Atlético Goianiense against Avaí on 8 October 2016.[12] He scored his first goal for the club against Tupi on 12 November 2016, scoring in the 87th minute.[13]
Paraná
[edit]Matheus made his league debut for Paraná against J. Malucelli on 12 February 2017.[14] He scored his first goal for the club against Rio Branco PR on 5 March 2017, scoring a penalty in the 37th minute.[15]
ABC
[edit]Matheus made his league debut for ABC against Santa Cruz on 28 April 2018.[16] He scored his first goal for the club against Santa Cruz on 23 May 2018, scoring in the 70th minute.[17]
First spell at Náutico
[edit]Matheus scored on his league debut against Central on 19 January 2019, scoring in the 59th minute.[18]
Botafogo SP
[edit]Matheus made his league debut against Santo André on 25 January 2022.[19] He scored his first goal for the club against Santos on 29 January 2022, scoring in the 62nd minute.[20]
Second spell at Náutico
[edit]Matheus scored on his league debut for Náutico against Central on 7 January 2023, scoring in the 33rd minute.[21]
Barra
[edit]Matheus made his league debut for Barra against Marcílio Dias on 28 January 2024.[22] He scored his first goal for Barra against Chapecoense on 31 January 2024, scoring in the 90th+5th minute.[23]
