Francesco Bonfiglio (born 20 January 1997) is an Italian football player who plays for Serie D club Notaresco.
Club career
[edit]He made his Serie C debut for Sicula Leonzio on 23 September 2017 in a game against Bisceglie.[1]
On 7 December 2018, he signed with Serie D club Marsala.[2] On 10 August, he then joined Marina di Ragusa.[3] He left the club in December 2019, to join SC Palazzolo.[4]
