Federico Ceccherini (born 11 May 1992) is an Italian professional footballer who plays as a centre back for Süper Lig club Fatih Karagümrük, on loan from Hellas Verona.[1]
Club career
[edit]On 12 July 2018, Ceccherini signed with Fiorentina.[2]
On 5 October 2020, he joined Hellas Verona on a season-long loan. Verona held an obligation to purchase his rights if certain conditions were met.[3]
On 15 August 2023, Ceccherini moved on a season-long loan to Fatih Karagümrük in Turkey.[4]
