Jonathan David Dos Santos Duré (born 18 April 1992) is a Uruguayan professional footballer who plays as a forward for Chilean club Everton. [1]
Career
[edit]Dos Santos returned to Cerro Largo for the 2019 season. He scored 13 goals in 32 league games for the club in his first season. At the end of December 2019 it was confirmed, that dos Santos would play for Peruvian side Universitario on loan for the 2020 season.[2]
In the second half of 2024, Dos Santos moved to Chile and joined Everton from Mushuc Runa.[3]
