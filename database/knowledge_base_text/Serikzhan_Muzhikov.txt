Serikzhan Urstemuly Muzhikov (Kazakh: Серікжан Урстемұлы Мұжықов, romanized: Serıkjan Urstemūly Mūjyqov; born 17 June 1989) is a Kazakhstan international football player who plays for Zhetysu in the Kazakhstan Premier League.[1]
Career
[edit]Muzhikov began his career in 2006 with FC Koksu. He has been playing for FC Zhetysu since 2008.
In July 2015, Muzhikov returned to FC Astana, signing an eighteen-month contract.[2] On 6 January 2020, Muzhikov left Astana at the end of his contract.[3] On 22 January 2020, FC Tobol announced the signing of Muzhikov on a one-year contract.[4]
