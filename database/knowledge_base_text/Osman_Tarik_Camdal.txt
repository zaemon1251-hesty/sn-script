Osman Tarık Çamdal (born 24 March 1991) is a Turkish professional footballer who plays as a full-back for Sarıyer.
International career
[edit]Çamdal made his debut for Turkey against the Republic of Ireland at the Aviva Stadium in Dublin. He scored the winning goal in a 2–1 victory; Turkey's first victory over Ireland on Irish soil.
Personal life
[edit]He married Swedish national midfielder Erkan Zengin's sister on 30 December 2015 in Södertälje, Sweden.[2][3]
