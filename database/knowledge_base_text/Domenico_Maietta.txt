Domenico Maietta (born 3 August 1982) is an Italian former professional footballer who played as a defender.
Career
[edit]Although Maietta was once included in the Serie A rosters of Juventus and Perugia in the early 2000s, he did not make his Serie A debut until 2013, after being promoted with Hellas Verona. He made his Serie B debut with Triestina during the 2002–03 season.[1]
Maietta missed 3 weeks in 2016–17 season.[2][3]
On 1 September 2020 Empoli announced his retirement from playing and that he joined the club staff.[4]
