Eray İşcan (born 19 July 1991) is a Turkish professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper.
Club career
[edit]İşcan made his debut in 2013. He was also at the starting lineup list at 2013–14 UEFA Champions League group stage matches against Copenhagen and Real Madrid.
