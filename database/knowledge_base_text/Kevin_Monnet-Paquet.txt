Kévin Monnet-Paquet (born 19 August 1988) is a French professional footballer who plays as a winger. Nicknamed Tuppy, he has represented France at under-18 and under-19 level.
Club career
[edit]Monnet-Paquet scored his first senior league goal with Lens against Valenciennes on 23 January 2008 in a 2–1 win away at the Stade Nungesser.[1]
On 19 August 2021, Monnet-Paquet joined Aris Limassol in Cyprus.[2]
Personal life
[edit]Monnet-Paquet's mother is of Rwandan descent and his father is French.[3]
