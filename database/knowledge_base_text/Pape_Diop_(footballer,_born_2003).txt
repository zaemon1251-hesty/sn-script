Pape Demba Diop (born 4 September 2003) is a Senegalese professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Challenger Pro League club Zulte Waregem and the Senegal national team.
Club career
[edit]Diop signed for Belgian side Zulte Waregem in September 2022, but would have to wait until October of the same year before he could train with his new side, due to work permit issues.[3][4][5]
International career
[edit]Diop was called up to the Senegalese under-20 side for the 2023 Africa U-20 Cup of Nations.[6] He scored in Senegal's 3–0 win over Mozambique, which sent Senegal through to the knockout stage.[7]
