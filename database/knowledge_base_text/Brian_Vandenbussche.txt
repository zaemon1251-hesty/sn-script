Brian Vandenbussche (Dutch pronunciation: [ˈbrɑi.ɑɱ vɑndɛmˈbʏsə];[1] born 24 September 1981) is a Belgian former professional footballer who played as a goalkeeper.
He formerly played for Sparta Rotterdam and ten seasons for Heerenveen.
Career
[edit]At the end of February 2019 it was confirmed, that Vandenbussche would join KSV Blankenberge ahead of the 2019–20 season.[2] Vandenbussche also became a youth coach and individual coach at the club.
