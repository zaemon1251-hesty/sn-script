Stefan Bell (German pronunciation: [ˈʃtɛfan ˈbɛl]; born 24 August 1991) is a German footballer who plays as a centre back or right-back for Bundesliga club Mainz 05.[1]
Career
[edit]In his early years, Bell played in the Jugendspielgemeinschaft Wehr/Rieden/Volkesfeld and afterwards he switched to the TuS Mayen. In 2007, he started playing in the youth team of 1. FSV Mainz 05. He started his professional career on 29 August 2010 with 1. FSV Mainz 05 where he is currently playing.[2]
