Davide Biraschi (born 2 July 1994) is an Italian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Serie B club Frosinone.
Club career
[edit]On 17 January 2022, Biraschi joined Fatih Karagümrük in Turkey on loan (renews).[1]
On 21 August 2024, Biraschi signed a two-season contract with Frosinone.[2]
Personal life
[edit]On 2 October 2020, Biraschi tested positive for COVID-19.[3]
