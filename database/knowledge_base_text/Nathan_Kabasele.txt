Nathan Clement Kabasele Mwamba, commonly known as Nathan Kabasele, (born 14 January 1994) is a Belgian professional footballer who is currently free agent and plays as a striker.[1]
International career
[edit]Kabasele was born in Belgium and is of Congolese descent.[2] He is a youth international footballer for Belgium.[3]
