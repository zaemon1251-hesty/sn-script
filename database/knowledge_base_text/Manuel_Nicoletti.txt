Manuel Nicoletti (born 9 December 1998) is an Italian footballer who plays for Serie C Group C club Picerno as a left back or left midfielder.[1]
Career
[edit]Nicoletti is a youth exponent from Crotone. He made his Serie B debut on 29 November 2014 against Modena. He replaced Bruno Martella after 90 minutes.[2]
He signed with Catanzaro on permanent basis on 20 July 2018 after spending the 2017–18 season on loan with the club.[3]
On 21 September 2020, he joined Monopoli on a two-year contract.[4]
On 3 August 2021, he signed a two-year contract for Foggia.[5]
On 31 January 2022, Nicoletti signed a contract with Crotone until 30 June 2024.[6] On 31 August 2022, he moved on loan to Reggiana, with an option to buy.[7] On 19 January 2024, Nicoletti was loaned to Casertana.[8]
On 28 August 2024, Nicoletti moved to Picerno.[9]
