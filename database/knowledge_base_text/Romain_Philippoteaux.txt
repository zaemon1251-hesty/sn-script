Romain Philippoteaux (French pronunciation: [ʁɔmɛ̃ filipɔto]; born 2 March 1988) is a former French professional footballer who played as a winger.
Career
[edit]On 28 June 2019, it was confirmed, that Philippoteaux had signed a three-year contract with Nîmes Olympique.[1]
On 3 September 2021, he returned to Dijon on a season-long loan.[2]
On 6 September 2022, he signed a two-year contract with Indian Super League club NorthEast United.[3]
He retired in 2024 at the age of 36.
