Jordan Ferri (born 12 March 1992) is a French professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Montpellier. He principally plays as a defensive midfielder and is known for his passing ability.[3]
Career
[edit]Ferri made his debut in the Europa League on 8 November 2012 against Athletic Bilbao, replacing Alexandre Lacazette in the 82nd minute. He made his league debut on 12 December against AS Nancy, replacing Anthony Réveillère after 30 minutes. On 22 November 2018, Ferri joined fellow Ligue 1 side Nîmes on loan until the end of the season.[4] At the beginning of the 2019–2020 season, Ferri joined Montpellier.
