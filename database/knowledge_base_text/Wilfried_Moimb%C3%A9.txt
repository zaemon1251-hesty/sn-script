Wilfried Moimbé-Tahrat (born 18 October 1988) is a French professional footballer who most recently played as a defender for Minnesota United in Major League Soccer.
Career
[edit]Moimbé played for the main squad of FC Girondins de Bordeaux on 19 December 2007 in a final group stage game of the 2007–08 UEFA Cup against Panionios F.C., coming on as a substitute in the 81st minute and scoring the winning goal on the 87th minute.
On 9 June 2015, Moimbé joined FC Nantes on a three-year deal.[1]
On 31 January 2018, Moimbé signed for Oldham Athletic on a short-term deal until the end of the 2017–2018 season.[2][3]
In September 2018, Moimbé signed with AS Nancy in French Ligue 2 as a free agent.[4]
On 23 July 2019, Moimbé joined MLS side Minnesota United.[5] He was released by Minnesota at the end of the season.[6]
Personal life
[edit]Born in Metropolitan France, Moimbé is of Reunion and Malian descent.[7] He is married to an Algerian native.[8]
