Franco Signorelli (born 1 January 1991) is a Venezuelan footballer who plays as a midfielder. He was awarded the award for the best goal in the Serie B 2011–12 season.
Club career
[edit]On 30 August 2019, he was released from his contract with Salernitana by mutual consent.[1]
On 7 February 2020, he signed with Serie C club Vibonese until the end of the 2019–20 season.[2]
On 21 August 2020 he joined newly promoted Serie C club Turris.[3]
