Cassio Cardoselli (born 26 July 1998) is an Italian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Serie C Group C club Sorrento.
Club career
[edit]Carrarese
[edit]On 3 September 2017, Cardoselli made his professional debut as a substitute replacing Francesco Tavano in the 89th minute of a 1–0 home win over Pro Piacenza.[1] On 17 September, Cardoselli played his first match as a starter, a 3–0 home win over Olbia, he was replaced by Daniel Kofi Agyei in the 82nd minute.[2] On 26 November he played his first entire match for Carrarese, a 4–1 home win over Pontedera.[3] On 24 March 2018, Cardoselli scored his first professional goal, as a substitute, in the 92nd minute of a 2–0 away win over Pisa.[4] Cardoselli ended his first professional season with 30 appearances, 1 goal and 2 assists.[5]
Entella
[edit]On 20 August 2020 he signed for Serie B side Virtus Entella.[6] On 12 August 2021, he was loaned to Siena.[7]
Virtus Francavilla
[edit]On 1 September 2022, Cardoselli joined Virtus Francavilla.[8]
Sorrento
[edit]On 9 August 2024, Cardoselli moved to Sorrento.[9]
