Guilherme Leal Siqueira (born 6 December 2004) is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a full-back for Sheffield Wednesday.[1]
Early and personal life
[edit]Possessing dual-citizenship of Brazil and Italy, Siqueira moved to the United Kingdom in childhood. He played in the Brentford academy until he was 12 years-old. After which, he joined the system at QPR, where he completed a two-year football scholarship.[2]
Career
[edit]He signed professional contact with Sheffield Wednesday in July 2023, following a trial with the club.[3] He began to be integrated with the Wednesday first-team match day squad during the 2023-24 season.[4] He made his professional debut with Sheffield Wednesday as a substitute in a 4–0 FA Cup win over Cardiff City on 6 January 2024.[5]
