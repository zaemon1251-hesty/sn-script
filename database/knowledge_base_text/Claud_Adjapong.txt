Claud Adjapong (born 6 May 1998) is an Italian professional footballer who plays as a right-back for Serie C Group B club Ascoli.[2]
Club career
[edit]Sassuolo
[edit]Adjapong is a youth exponent from Sassuolo. He made his Serie A debut on 11 March 2016 against Juventus. He replaced Matteo Politano after 89 minutes in a 1–0 away defeat.[3]
Loan to Hellas Verona
[edit]On 21 August 2019, Adjapong joined Serie A club Verona on loan with an option to buy.[4]
Loan to Reggina
[edit]On 9 August 2021, he moved to Serie B side Reggina on a season-long loan.[5]
Ascoli
[edit]On 1 September 2022, Adjapong signed a three-year contract with Ascoli.[6]
International career
[edit]Adjapong was born and raised in Italy to parents of Ghanaian descent. Until he was 18, he only had a Ghanaian passport. He is eligible to play for the Ghana national team.[7][8]
After receiving his passport, on 25 August 2016, he received a call-up from the Italy-U19.[9][10] He made his debut with the Italy-U21 team on 1 September 2017, in a friendly match lost 3–0 against Spain.
