Eros De Santis (born 30 October 1997) is an Italian professional footballer who plays as a right-back for Serie C Group C club Team Altamura.
Club career
[edit]De Santis made his Serie B debut for Virtus Entella on 24 October 2017, in a game against Cremonese.[2]
On 31 January 2019, Monza announced the signing of De Santis on loan from Siena, with an option to make the deal permanent at the end of the season.[3]
On 16 January 2020, he joined Serie C club Viterbese.[4]
International career
[edit]De Santis was a youth international for Italy.[5]
