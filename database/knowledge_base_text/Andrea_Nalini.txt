Andrea Nalini (born 20 June 1990) is an Italian footballer who plays as a forward for Mozzecane.[1][2]
Club career
[edit]Nalini started his career with Virtus Verona, playing at the Serie D level for a number of years. In 2013, after the club's promotion to professionalism, he was scouted by Salernitana and signed by them. In 2016, he signed for newly-promoted Serie A side Crotone, making his debut in the top flight. In the final game of the 2016–17 season, he scored a brace (his only two Serie A goals to date) that led Crotone to a vital 2–1 win to Lazio that allowed the Calabrians to escape relegation.
On 31 January 2020, he signed a 1.5-year contract with Vicenza.[3]
On 14 September 2021 he returned to Virtus Verona.[4]
