Christian Strohdiek (born 22 January 1988) is a German professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for SC Paderborn II.[1]
Club career
[edit]In May 2022, following relegation from the 3. Liga with Würzburger Kickers Strohdiek announced his retirement from playing.[2] However, he returned for the 2022–23 season, joining SC Paderborn II in the fifth-tier Oberliga Westfalen.
