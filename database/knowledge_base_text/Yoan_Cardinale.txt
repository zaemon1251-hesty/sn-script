Yoan Cardinale (born 27 March 1994) is a French professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for SC Toulon.
Career
[edit]Cardinale is a youth product of Nice. He made his Ligue 1 debut on 18 October 2015 against Rennes, playing the full game.[1] He played for Ligue 1 club Nice until the end of the 2020–21 season.[2]
On 24 August 2022, Cardinale joined SC Toulon on a free transfer, after a year without a club.[3]
