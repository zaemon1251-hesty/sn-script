Lars Bender (German pronunciation: [ˈlaʁs ˈbɛndɐ]; born 8 January 1988) is a German footballer who plays as a midfielder for Fortuna Köln II in Mittelrheinliga.[1]
Career
[edit]Bender made his debut on the professional league level in the 2. Bundesliga for TuS Koblenz on 22 August 2008, when he came on as a substitute in the 67th minute in a game against FSV Frankfurt. On 16 June 2011, he joined Kickers Offenbach.
