Maksim Anatolyevich Zhavnerchik (Belarusian: Максім Анатольевіч Жаўнерчык; Russian: Максим Анатольевич Жавнерчик; born 9 February 1985) is a Belarusian former football defender.
Career
[edit]Zhavnerchik received his first call-up to the national side for the friendly match against Kazakhstan, which was held on 9 February 2011, but remained an unused substitute for the game. On 29 March 2011, Zhavnerchik made his debut for the team, playing the first half in a friendly against Canada.
In January 2015, six-years after leaving BATE Borisov for Kuban Krasnodar, Zhavnerchik re-signed for BATE Borisov.[1]
Honours
[edit]BATE Borisov
- Belarusian Premier League champion: 2006, 2007, 2008, 2015, 2016, 2017
- Belarusian Cup winner: 2005–06, 2014–15
- Belarusian Super Cup winner: 2015, 2016, 2017
