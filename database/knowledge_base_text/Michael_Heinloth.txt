Michael Heinloth (born 9 February 1992) is a German professional footballer who plays as a right-back.[1]
Club career
[edit]Heinloth joined SC Paderborn 07 in 2014 from 1. FC Nürnberg. He made his Bundesliga debut at 20 September 2014 against Hannover 96 in a 2–0 home win. He played the first 71 minutes, before being substituted by Stefan Kutschke.[2]
On 18 July 2016, he signed a two-year contract at NEC Nijmegen.[3]
On 20 July 2018, he signed a year contract at Zagłębie Sosnowiec. He made his debut in an Ekstraklasa match against Zagłębie Lubin on 3 October 2018.
