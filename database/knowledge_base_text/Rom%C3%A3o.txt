Luiz Fernando Borges Romão, simply Romão (born 31 July 1989), is a Brazilian footballer who plays for Capivariano as a forward.
Career
[edit]Born in Jaú, Romão began his career on XV de Jaú, and went on to play for a host of clubs. In May 2013, he signed a contract with Série A outfit Portuguesa.,[1] and made his top flight debut on 25th, in a 0-1 defeat against Vasco, as a starter.[2]
