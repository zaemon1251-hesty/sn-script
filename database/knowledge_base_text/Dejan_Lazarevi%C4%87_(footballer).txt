Dejan Lazarević (born 15 February 1990) is a Slovenian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Slovenian PrvaLiga club Domžale.
Club career
[edit]Jagiellonia Białystok
[edit]On 18 January 2018, Lazarević signed a two-and-a-half-year contract with Jagiellonia Białystok.[1]
Legnago Salus
[edit]On 31 January 2021, he joined Italian third-tier Serie C club Legnago Salus.[2]
International career
[edit]Having represented Slovenia at all youth levels from under-17 to under-21,[3] Lazarević made his debut for the senior team on 15 November 2011 in a friendly match against the United States, replacing Armin Bačinović late in the game.[4] Overall, he earned a total of 20 caps, scoring 1 goal until his final cap in 2015.[5]
