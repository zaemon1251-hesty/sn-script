Antonijo Ježina (born 5 June 1989) is a Croatian footballer who most recently played for Osijek as a goalkeeper.
Club career
[edit]Ježina joined Belgian second tier-side Royal Antwerp in summer 2016.[1]
International career
[edit]He made his debut for the Croatian senior national team on September 10, 2013, in a friendly match against South Korea.[2] Entering the match as an injury time substitute for Dario Krešić, he conceded a goal in less than a minute he spent on the field.[3]
