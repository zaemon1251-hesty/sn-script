Tomás Mascarenhas Lemos Reymão Nogueira (born 14 July 1998) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Liga Portugal 2 club Leixões. As a youth player, he spent time at clubs such as Sporting CP, Chelsea, Wolverhampton Wanderers and Fiorentina.[1]
Club career
[edit]Reymão made his professional debut with Boavista F.C. in a 1-0 Primeira Liga loss to C.S. Marítimo on 8 July 2020.[2] In the axadrezada team,[3] despite his youth and the change of coaches, he has been consolidating his position in the squad, now in Petit's options, and he is one of the current great hopes of the Black Panthers' supporters.
On 23 July 2022, Reymão moved abroad and joined Spanish Segunda División side Albacete Balompié on a one-year contract.[4] The following 31 January, after just one cup match, he terminated his contract.[5]
On 16 October 2023, Reymão signed for Liga Portugal 2 club Leixões.[6]
