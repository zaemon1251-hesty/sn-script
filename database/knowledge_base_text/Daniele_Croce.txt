Daniele Croce (born 9 September 1982) is an Italian former footballer who played as a central midfielder.
Club career
[edit]Pescara
[edit]Born in Giulianova, Abruzzo, Croce started his career at Abruzzese club Pescara.
Arezzo
[edit]Croce was signed by fellow Serie B club Arezzo in a co-ownership deal for €600,000 on 30 August 2006.[1][2]
In June 2007 Arezzo signed Croce outright for another €431,000.[1] Arezzo folded in 2010.
Alessandria & Sorrento
[edit]In 2010 Croce joined Alessandria. In 2011, he was signed by Sorrento.[3]
Empoli
[edit]In 2012, he returned to Serie B with Empoli F.C. The club promoted back to Serie A in 2014. On 24 May 2016 he signed a new 1-year contract with the club.[4]
Cremonese
[edit]After Empoli relegated in 2017, Croce joined Serie B club Cremonese on a free transfer.[5]
