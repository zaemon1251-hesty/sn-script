Gabriel Barbosa Avelino (born 17 March 1999), known as Gabriel Barbosa, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a striker for Liga Portugal 2 club Penafiel.
Career
[edit]In June 2021, Palmeiras sent Barbosa to FC Seoul on loan.[2]
