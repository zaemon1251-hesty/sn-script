Bruno Batista Pereira Pires (born 12 May 1992), known as Bruno Pirri, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a central defender.[2][1]
Club career
[edit]On 6 September 2020, Pires joined China League One side Kunshan.[3] On 4 August 2022, he moved to Spanish club CD Lugo in Segunda División, on a one-year deal.[4]
