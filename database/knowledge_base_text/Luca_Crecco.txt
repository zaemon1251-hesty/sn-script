Luca Crecco (born 6 September 1995) is an Italian footballer who plays as a left winger for Serie C Group C club Latina.
Club career
[edit]Crecco scored his first professional career goal with Lazio on 23 April 2017, in a 6–2 home win over Palermo.[1]
On 7 August 2018, Crecco signed with Serie B club Pescara.[2] On 29 January 2021, he was loaned to Cosenza.[3] On 15 July 2021, he joined Vicenza on loan with an option to buy.[4]
On 24 January 2023, Crecco signed a three-year contract with Taranto.[5]
On 17 August 2023, Crecco signed for Latina on loan for the season.[6]
On 13 August 2024, Crecco signed for Latina, this time permanentely.[7]
