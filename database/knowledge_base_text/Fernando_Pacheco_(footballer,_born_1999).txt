Fernando José Pacheco Rivas (born 26 June 1999) is a Peruvian professional footballer who plays for Deportivo Municipal on loan from Sporting Cristal, as a winger.
Club career
[edit]Sporting Cristal
[edit]Born in Bujama Baja, Mala, Cañete, Pacheco joined Sporting Cristal's youth setup at the age of 14.[1] He made his first team debut on 20 August 2016 at the age of just 17, starting in a 1–0 home win against Unión Comercio.[2]
Pacheco scored his first professional goal on 3 March 2018, in a 5–0 routing of Universidad San Martín de Porres.[3]
Fluminense
[edit]On 13 January 2020, Pacheco moved abroad and signed a four-year contract with Campeonato Brasileiro Série A side Fluminense.[4]
Emmen
[edit]On 4 July 2022, Pacheco joined Emmen in the Netherlands on a season-long loan.[5]
