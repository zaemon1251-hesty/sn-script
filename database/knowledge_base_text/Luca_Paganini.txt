Luca Paganini (born 8 June 1993) is an Italian footballer who plays as a right winger for Serie C Group B club Vis Pesaro.[1]
Club career
[edit]Paganini is a youth exponent from Frosinone Calcio. He made his first team debut on 18 December 2011 against U.S. Siracusa in a Lega Pro Prima Divisione game. He came in as an 89th-minute substitute for Fabio Catacchini in a 1-0 away defeat.[2] He was loaned out to Fondi for six months from January 2013 until June 2013. He scored his first Serie A goal on 18 October 2015 with Frosinone after a 2–0 defeat of Sampdoria.
On 7 September 2020, he joined Lecce.[3]
On 27 January 2022, he signed with Ascoli until the end of the 2021–22 season.[4]
On 24 August 2022, Paganini signed a two-year contract with Triestina.[5]
On 4 July 2024, he moved to Vis Pesaro on a two-year deal.[6]
