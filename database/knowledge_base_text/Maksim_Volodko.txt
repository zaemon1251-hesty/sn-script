Maksim Valadzko (Belarusian: Максім Валадзько; Russian: Максим Володько; born 10 November 1992) is a Belarusian professional footballer who plays for Maltese side Floriana.[1]
Club career
[edit]On 12 January 2019, he signed with the Russian Premier League club FC Arsenal Tula.[2] He made his debut for Arsenal on 4 May 2019 in a game against FC Lokomotiv Moscow as a 76th-minute substitute for Luka Đorđević.[3] He left Arsenal on 31 July 2020.[4]
On 14 October 2020 he joined FC Tambov.[5]
International
[edit]Maksim Valadzko made his debut for the senior national side of his country on 18 November 2014, in the 3:2 home win over Mexico in a friendly match, playing 76 minutes.[6]
Honours
[edit]BATE Borisov
- Belarusian Premier League champion: 2009, 2011, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018
- Belarusian Cup winner: 2014–15, 2020–21
- Belarusian Super Cup winner: 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2022
