Tohouri Zahoui Constant Djakpa (born 17 October 1986) is an Ivorian professional footballer who plays as a left back.
Career
[edit]On 12 September 2018, Djakpa joined Regionalliga Südwest side Hessen Dreieich.[2]
International career
[edit]Djakpa played for the national Olympic team and for the Ivory Coast U21. He represented his country at the 2008 Toulon Tournament, scoring two goals, one a penalty against the United States,[3] and one against Chile.[4]
Represented the team at 2014 World Cup.[5]
