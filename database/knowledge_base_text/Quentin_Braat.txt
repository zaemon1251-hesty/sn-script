Quentin Braat (born 6 July 1997) is a French professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Segunda División club Oviedo.
Professional career
[edit]On 25 May 2017, Braat signed his first professional contract with FC Nantes.[1] On 30 June 2019, he went on loan to Niort for the 2019-20 Ligue 2 season.[2] Braat made his professional debut with Niort in a 2-0 Ligue 2 loss to Troyes AC on 26 July 2019.[3] He left Niort at the end of the 2021–22 season having made 55 appearances for the club in all competitions.[4]
On 1 July 2022, Braat signed a three-year contract with Oviedo in Spain.[5]
