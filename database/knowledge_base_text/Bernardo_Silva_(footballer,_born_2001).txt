Bernardo Neves Jesus Gouveia Silva (born 31 May 2001), known as Benny Silva or simply Benny, is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays for Liga 3 club 1º Dezembro. Mainly a defensive midfielder, he can also play as a central midfielder or centre-back.
Career
[edit]Benny made his professional debut for Dordrecht on 27 February 2021 in the Eerste Divisie.[1]
On 16 September 2021, he returned to Portugal and signed with Famalicão.[2]
In September 2022, Famalicão sent Benny on a season-long loan to Liga 3 side Real SC.[3]
In September 2023, after his contract with Famalicão had expired, Benny kept playing in Liga 3, signing a one-year deal with 1º Dezembro.[4]
