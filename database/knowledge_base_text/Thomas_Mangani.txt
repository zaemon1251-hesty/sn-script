Thomas Mathieu Romain Mangani (born 29 April 1987) is a French professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Ligue 2 club Ajaccio.
Career
[edit]In summer 2014, Mangani left France to play for Chievo, signing a two-year contract with the Serie A club on 6 July 2014.[2] However, after a disappointing season in Italy, where he only played 45 minutes for Chievo, Mangani returned to France with Angers, with whom he signed a three-year deal on 4 July 2015.[citation needed]
On 13 June 2022, Mangani signed a two-year contract with newly-promoted Ligue 1 side Ajaccio, returning to the club after fourteen years.[3][4]
Mangani holds both French and Italian nationalities.[5]
