Filippo Costa (born 21 May 1995) is an Italian footballer who plays as a left midfielder for Serie C Group A club Vicenza.
Club career
[edit]Costa started his career in his native Veneto club Chievo. In July 2015 he was signed by English club Bournemouth.[1] However, in January 2016 he returned to Chievo.
In January 2017 he was loaned to SPAL with an option to buy.[2] In June 2017 SPAL excised the option for €1 million transfer fee.[3]
On 13 July 2019, Costa signed to Napoli. On 15 July 2019, he joined Bari on loan until 30 June 2021.[4][5] On 18 September 2020, Napoli terminated the Bari loan and sent him on loan to Serie B club Virtus Entella.[6] On 10 January 2022, he joined Parma in Serie B on loan until the end of the season, with an option to buy.[7] On 1 September 2022, Costa was loaned to Foggia.[8]
On 19 July 2023, Costa signed a two-year contract with Vicenza.[9]
