Uche Agbo (born 4 April 1975) is a retired Nigerian professional football midfielder. He played for several clubs in Europe.

Career

[edit]

Agbo played in FR Yugoslavia for two Belgrade clubs FK Obilić and FK Rad[1] before moving to clubs in France and Turkey.[2] He scored one goal for Obilić in the First League of FR Yugoslavia, on 26 November 1995, in a away defeat against FK Sloboda Užice by 1–2.[3] He played for Montpellier HSC in the French Ligue 1 during the 1997–98 season[4][5] and Adanaspor in the Turkish Süper Lig during the 1998–99 season.[6]
