Mirko Eramo (born 12 July 1989) is an Italian professional football player who plays as a midfielder.

Club career

[edit]

On 31 January 2020, he signed a 2.5-year contract with Ascoli.[1] On 15 September 2023, Eramo's contract with Ascoli was terminated by mutual consent.[2]
