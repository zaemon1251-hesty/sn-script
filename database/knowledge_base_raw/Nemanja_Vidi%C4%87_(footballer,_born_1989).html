Nemanja Vidić (Serbian Cyrillic: Немања Видић, pronounced [němaɲa ʋǐːditɕ]; born 6 August 1989) is a Serbian professional footballer who plays for China League One club Heilongjiang Ice City as a defensive midfielder.[2][3]

Club career

[edit]

In 2017, he played in Malaysia for Negeri Sembilan.[4]

On 29 July 2020, Vidić transferred to China League One club Sichuan Jiuniu.[5]

On 15 April 2022, Vidić transferred to China League One club Nanjing City.[6]

On 19 April 2023, Vidić transferred to China League One club Heilongjiang Ice City.[7]
