Yunus Mallı (born 24 February 1992) is a professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Süper Lig club Konyaspor and the Turkey national team.[1] Born in Germany, and a former German youth international, he plays for the Turkey national team.

Club career

[edit]

On 5 January 2017, Mallı joined VfL Wolfsburg after signing a 4+1⁄2-year deal.[2]

In July 2022, Mallı signed with Kasımpaşa.[3]

On 1 August 2023, he signed with the Süper Lig club Konyaspor.[4]

International career

[edit]

Mallı has 30 caps for Germany youth teams, including 11 for Germany U21. He made his debut on 11 November 2013, playing the second half in a win against Montenegro U21. On 6 November 2015, Mallı was selected for the Turkey national team to play friendlies against Qatar and Greece respectively.[5] He was part of Euro 2016 squad of the Turkey national football team.[6]
