Gaëtan Robail (born 9 January 1994) is a French professional footballer who plays as a left winger for Challenger Pro League club RWD Molenbeek.

Career

[edit]

On 5 October 2020, Robail joined Guingamp on a season-long loan from Lens.[1][2] On 12 January 2021, Robail moved to Valenciennes on 18-month loan deal with an option to sign permanently.[3]

On 22 July 2022, Robail signed a two-year contract with Atromitos in Greece.[4]

On 3 July 2024, Robail signed for Challenger Pro League club RWD Molenbeek.[5]
