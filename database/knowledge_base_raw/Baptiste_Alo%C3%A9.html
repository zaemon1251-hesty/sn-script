Baptiste Aloé (born 29 June 1994) is a French professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Championnat National 2 club SAS Épinal.

Club career

[edit]

Marseille

[edit]

Aloé was born in La Ciotat, Bouches-du-Rhône and as a young boy played for his local club, E.S. de La Ciotat, before joining Marseille in 2003.[2][3] He made his professional debut on 4 October 2012 in the 2012–13 Europa League campaign against Cypriot side AEL Limassol in a 5–1 home win. He played the full 90 minutes as a right back.[4] He made his first appearance in Ligue 1 as a second-half substitute in the visit to Lens on 2 November 2014, and played the whole of the 3–1 win at home to Bordeaux three weeks later.[1]

On 6 March 2015, he made his first professional goal against Toulouse FC. During this period, he took part in fourteen matches.

Loans to Valenciennes

[edit]

In late August 2015, Aloé was loaned out to Ligue 2 club Valenciennes.[5]

At the end of October 2016, Aloé had to undergo meniscus surgery and was ruled out from football for at least a month.[6]

Valenciennes

[edit]

After two years of being on loan, Aloé signed on with a three-year contract.

Dinamo București

[edit]

On 3 February 2022, he signed a contract with Romanian side Dinamo București.[7]

Nancy

[edit]

On 15 July 2022, Aloé joined Nancy on a two-year deal.[8]

International career

[edit]

Aloé played twice for France at under-18 level in 2012.

Aloé played once for France at under-21 level in 2015.
