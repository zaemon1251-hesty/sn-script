Jules Hermon Aziz Iloki (born 14 January 1992) is a French professional footballer who last played as a winger for Chinese Super League club Tianjin Jinmen Tiger.

Early life

[edit]

Iloki was born in the 12th arrondissement of Paris, to a Guinean mother born in Conakry. He acquired the French nationality on 27 June 1996, through the collective effect of his mother's naturalization.[1]

Club career

[edit]

Iloki made his Ligue 1 debut at 25 October 2014 against Evian Thonon Gaillard in a 2–0 away win. He replaced Vincent Bessat after 73 minutes.[2]

On 30 July 2020, Iloki transferred to China League One club Sichuan Jiuniu.[3] On 22 October 2020, Iloki was loaned to fellow China League One club Suzhou Dongwu.[4]

International career

[edit]

Iloki was called up to the Congo national football team in 2015.[5]
