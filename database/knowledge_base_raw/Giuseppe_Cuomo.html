Giuseppe Cuomo (born 2 February 1998) is an Italian footballer who plays for Serie C Group A side Vicenza as a defender.[2]

Career

[edit]

Cuomo made his professional debut for Crotone in a Serie A 3–0 loss to Napoli on 12 March 2017.[3] He stayed with Crotone through their relegation to Serie B and then Serie C.

On 7 August 2023, Cuomo signed a three-year contract with Südtirol.[4] On 10 January 2024, he was loaned to Vicenza, with an option to buy.[5] On 13 June 2024, Vicenza exercised the option and signed a two-season contract with Cuomo.[6]
