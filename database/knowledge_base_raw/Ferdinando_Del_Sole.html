Ferdinando Del Sole (born 17 January 1998) is an Italian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Serie C Group B club Pineto.

Club career

[edit]

Pescara

[edit]

Del Sole is a youth exponent from Pescara. He made his Serie B debut on 27 August 2017 against Foggia.[2]

Loan to Juve Stabia

[edit]

On 15 July 2019, he joined Serie B side Juve Stabia on loan from Juventus.[3]

Juventus U23

[edit]

On 19 January 2020, Del Sole played his first game for Juventus U23, the reserve team of Juventus, in a 1–0 Serie C win against Arezzo.[4]

Potenza

[edit]

On 1 September 2022, Del Sole was loaned to Potenza for two seasons.[5]

Pineto

[edit]

On 4 July 2024, Del Sole signed a multi-year contract with Pineto.[6]
