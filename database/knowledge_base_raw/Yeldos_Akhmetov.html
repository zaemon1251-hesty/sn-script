Yeldos Kuanyshuly Akhmetov (Kazakh: Елдос Қуанышұлы Ахметов, Eldos Quanyşūly Ahmetov; born 1 June 1990) is a former Kazakh footballer who plays as a defender for Aksu in the Kazakhstan Premier League.

Career

[edit]

On 18 November 2016, Akhmetov signed a three-year contract with FC Kairat.[1] On 11 January 2020, FC Kaisar announced the signing of Akhmetov.[2]
