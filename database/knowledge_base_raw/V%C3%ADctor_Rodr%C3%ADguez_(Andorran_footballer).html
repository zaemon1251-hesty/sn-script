Víctor Rodríguez Soria (born 7 September 1987) is an Andorran retired international footballer who played as a defender. Rodríguez made his international debut in 2008.

Career

[edit]

On 6 September 2015, in a 3–0 away loss to Bosnia and Herzegovina in UEFA Euro 2016 qualifying, Muhamed Bešić threw his chewing gum at Rodríguez, who retaliated; both were given straight red cards.[1]
