Luciano
PronunciationSpanish: [luˈθjano], Latin American Spanish: [luˈsjano]
Portuguese: [lusiˈɐnu]
Italian: [luˈtʃaːno]
GenderMale
Language(s)Spanish, Portuguese, Italian.
Other names
Related namesLuciana, Lucian

Luciano is an Italian, Spanish and Portuguese given name and surname.[1][2] It is derived from Latin Lucianus, patronymic of Lucius ("Light"). The French form is Lucien, while the Basque form is Luken.
