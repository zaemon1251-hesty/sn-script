Yanni Regäsel (born 13 January 1996) is a German professional footballer who plays as a right-back for Oberliga Westfalen club TuS Bövinghausen.

Club career

[edit]

Regäsel made his professional debut for Hertha BSC on 31 October 2015, in a Bundesliga match against Borussia Mönchengladbach in Olympiastadion.[1]

He moved to Eintracht Frankfurt on 1 February 2016.[2]

On 30 May 2018, he was signed by MSV Duisburg for the 2018–19 season.[3] He left Duisburg after the 2018–19 season.[4]

International career

[edit]

Regäsel is a youth international for Germany.[5]
