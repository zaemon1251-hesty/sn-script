From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
For other uses, see Silvio (disambiguation).

Silvio (Italian: [ˈsilvjo], Spanish: [ˈsilbjo]) is an Italian male name, the male equivalent of Silvia. Sílvio is a variant of the name in Portuguese. It is derived from the Latin "Silvius", meaning "spirit of the wood," and may refer to:

  • Silvio De Sousa (born 1998), Angolan basketball player

  • Silvio Frondizi (1907–1974), Argentine lawyer

  • Silvio Gesell (1862–1930), German economist
