Vincent Edouard André Koziello (born 28 October 1995) is a French footballer who plays as a midfielder for Belgian Challenger Pro League club Oostende.[1]

Club career

[edit]

Koziello is a youth exponent from OGC Nice. He made his Ligue 1 debut on 1 November 2014 against Olympique Lyonnais, replacing Grégoire Puel after 72 minutes in a 1–3 home defeat.[2]

In January 2018, he joined Bundesliga side 1. FC Köln on a 4+1⁄2-year contract until 2022. The transfer paid to Nice was reported as €3.3 million plus potential bonuses.[3][4]

On 28 April 2018, he came off the bench as Köln lost 3–2 to SC Freiburg which confirmed Köln’s relegation from the Bundesliga.[5]

On 11 January 2023, Koziello was loaned by Virton.[6]

Personal life

[edit]

Koziello was born in France, and is of Polish descent through his paternal grandfather.[7] He is a youth international for France.
