Artur Oleksiyovych Rudko (Ukrainian: Артур Олексійович Рудько; born 7 May 1992) is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Ukrainian Premier League side Chornomorets Odesa.

Career

[edit]

On 1 July 2024, Rudko joined Ukrainian Premier League side Chornomorets Odesa,[2] making his debut against Kryvbas Kryvyi Rih on 3 August 2024.[3]

Honours

[edit]

Dynamo Kyiv

  • Ukrainian Premier League: 2015–16
  • Ukrainian Cup: 2013–14, 2014–15
  • Ukrainian Super Cup: 2016, 2018, 2019
