Víctor Ruiz Abril (born 2 November 1993) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for Swiss club St. Gallen as a forward.

Club career

[edit]

Born in Utiel, Ruiz graduated from the youth academy of local Valencia CF and made his senior debut with CD Utiel in the 2012–13 season, in Tercera División. On 26 July 2014, he moved to Segunda División B side CD Olímpic de Xàtiva.[1] On 19 April 2015, he scored his first goal for the club in a 2–2 draw against Villarreal CF B.[2] On 13 July 2015, he moved to Valencia CF Mestalla in the same tier.[3]

On 17 September 2016, Ruiz returned to Tercera after signing with UD Alzira.[4] In the following two seasons, he continued his career in the same tier with Yeclano Deportivo and SD Formentera.[5]

On 9 December 2018, Ruiz moved abroad and joined Swiss Super League club St. Gallen on a contract running from 2019 until the summer of 2021.[6] On 2 June 2020, he extended his contract with the club until 2023.[7]

On 2 August 2022, Ruiz joined Saudi Arabian club Al-Fayha on a two-year deal.[8]

On 14 February 2024, Ruiz returned to St. Gallen until the end of the season.[9]
