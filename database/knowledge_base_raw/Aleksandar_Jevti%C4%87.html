Aleksandar Jevtić (Serbian Cyrillic: Aлекcaндap Jeвтић, pronounced [aleksǎːndar jěːʋtitɕ, alěksaːn-];[1][2] born 30 March 1985) is a Serbian retired football striker .[3]
